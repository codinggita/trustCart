## 📌 Problem Statement

Consumers face a double-blind shopping problem:

- ❌ Cannot check real-time product availability in nearby stores → wasted trips  
- ⚠️ Cannot verify authenticity of products → risk of fake or expired items  

---

## 💡 Solution

TrustCart solves this by providing:

- 🔍 Live local product availability  
- ✅ Verified product authenticity  
- 📦 Batch & expiry transparency  
- 📍 Nearby store insights

## 🎯 Features

### 🏠 Home Page
- Smart product search

- Feature highlights

### 🔍 Search Results
- Filter by price, brand, distance
- Verified products badge
- Availability across stores

### 🏪 Store Availability
- Nearby store listing
- Stock status (In Stock / Low / Out)
